BoostHub Account Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your BoostHub account and associated app data.

Request Account Deletion

Email BoostHub support from the email address connected to your account.

Email:
support@boosthubapp.com

Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address used for your BoostHub account.

Data Deleted

After we verify the request, we will delete or anonymize account data associated with your BoostHub account, including:

  • Account profile information
  • Vehicle builds and build details
  • Uploaded build photos and dyno sheets
  • Comments, likes, follows, and notifications
  • Reports, feedback, and other account-associated activity

Retention

Some limited records may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or operational reasons. Data retained for these purposes will be kept only as long as necessary.

In-App Deletion

If available in your app version, you can also delete your account inside BoostHub by going to Profile and using Delete Account.

For privacy questions, contact support@boosthubapp.com.
