Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  |  BoostHub LLC  |  Wisconsin, USA

BoostHub LLC ("BoostHub," "we," "us," or "our") operates the BoostHub mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information: Username, display name, bio, and avatar photo you choose to provide.
  • Build information: Vehicle details, photos, dyno sheets, horsepower numbers, mod lists, and location data you submit.
  • Usage data: Likes, comments, follows, and other interactions within the app.
  • Device information: Push notification tokens to deliver notifications to your device.
  • Crash reports: Anonymized crash and error data via Sentry to help us fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the BoostHub service.
  • To display your public profile and builds to other users.
  • To send push notifications about activity on your builds.
  • To enable community features such as likes, comments, and follows.
  • To respond to support requests and feedback.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, and violations of our Terms of Use.
  • To improve and maintain the app using anonymized crash data.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Build photos and dyno sheets are stored in Supabase Storage. All data is stored in the United States.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database and storage), Sentry (crash reporting), Resend (transactional email), and Expo (push notifications). These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of BoostHub or its users.

5. Public Content

Builds you mark as public, your username, display name, avatar, and any comments or likes you make are visible to other users of BoostHub. You control whether your build appears on BoostMap via the public/private setting on each build.

6. Age Requirement

BoostHub is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 16. If we become aware that a user is under 16, we will delete their account and data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data. You can do this directly in the app under Profile → Delete Account, or by contacting us.
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format. This is available in-app under Profile → My Data.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@boosthubapp.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete your profile, builds, photos, comments, likes, and all associated data within 30 days. Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes.

9. Push Notifications

We use Expo's push notification service to deliver notifications about activity on your builds. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings or within the BoostHub app under Profile → Notifications.

10. DMCA — Copyright Policy

BoostHub respects intellectual property rights. If you believe content on BoostHub infringes your copyright, please send a DMCA takedown notice to dmca@boosthubapp.com with the following information:

  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
  • The URL or location of the allegedly infringing content.
  • Your contact information.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized.
  • Your electronic or physical signature.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The BoostHub mobile app does not use cookies. Our landing page uses Google Analytics to track aggregate page visits. No personal data is shared with Google Analytics.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of BoostHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin, United States, without regard to conflict of law principles.
